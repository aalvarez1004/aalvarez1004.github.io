"Air pollution in Mataró"


In this graph we can see the variation of pollutants over the years in the city of Mataró, from 1999 to 2023.
This project aims to analyze and understand the evolution of air quality in Mataró during this period of time, we will do it with different components with its image and text correxpondiente to analyze them in detail and to compare each one to get more information about the work.
